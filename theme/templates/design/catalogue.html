Design catalogue Live · contributors

A live view of the design system, rendered by the app itself — the tokens are the real compiled CSS values and the components are the real template partials, so this page can't drift from what ships. It's reachable only by typing /design (no nav link anywhere) and is meant for contributors.

The narrative user-journey docs (build-a-model, onboarding, view-results, audit-a-result, compare-models, save-and-load) and the design rationale live in specs/design/ in the repo — they're version-controlled documentation, reviewed in PRs. This route is the complementary live surface for tokens and components.

Live, not a mirror. Change a token in theme/static/scss/custom.scss (recompiled to bs_main.css) or a component partial under model_builder/templates/, and this page updates automatically — nothing to hand-sync.
