Do you know the environmental footprint of your digital service?

Model it, simulate changes before you make them, and prioritize the actions that actually reduce it.

Documentation

What is e-footprint?

e-footprint is an open-source tool that models the environmental footprint of digital services, web or edge, so teams can compare scenarios and prioritize where to act before they build.

Who is this for?

For the whole team behind a digital service or device fleet: engineers, designers, product managers and architects, alongside the operational sustainability leads who work with them, on web and on industrial or edge systems. Approachable for non-code roles, with depth on demand. A team takes about 30 minutes on average to model a first service.

How does it work?

Define your digital services

Describe your service: usage journeys, functionalities, volumes and geographies, and the infrastructure they run on.

Analyze environmental impact

Get a breakdown of the impact across the whole system.

Simulate & prioritize

Compare scenarios and find the highest-impact actions before you build.

About us

e-footprint is part of the Green IT association Boavizta's ecosystem of open-source tools, initiated by Publicis Sapient. It is a community-driven project dedicated to improving digital sustainability through transparent, decision-focused environmental modeling.
